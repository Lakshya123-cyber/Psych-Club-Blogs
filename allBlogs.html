About Us

We are a group of 16-17 yr olds from Global Indian International School who share a keen interest in Psychology. We aim to educate people about psychology and its importance in our lives. We are a dedicated group of people who have involved ourselves in community service and we are also open to collaborating with external psychology clubs or foundations.
